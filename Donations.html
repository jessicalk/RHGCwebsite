Donation Requests

River Hill Garden Center supports local charities, especially those that support our local community or are aligned with our mission of creating a cleaner, healthier community. Local 501(c)(3) organizations are given priority.

See the organizations we supported in 2014.

We receive many inquiries and although we can't fill them all we welcome your request. Please submit your donation or charity request at least 30 to 60 days in advance. Be as specific as possible with your request to help us understand your needs and make a decision promptly.

River Hill Garden Center will notify you if your request can be accommodated. Due to the large volume of requests we receive, we are not able to reply with the status of each request or to accept telephone or in-person requests. We appreciate the goals of each organization and commend you for your efforts in charity and giving.
