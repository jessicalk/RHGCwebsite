Christmas Shop

The holidays are our favorite time of year. Our hardworking elves come together to transform the entire garden center into the largest Christmas Shop in Howard County. We carry everything you need to decorate, entertain, and celebrate during the holidays. You'll find a large assortment of fresh trees, decorated wreathes, ornaments, collectibles, silk and fresh flowers, lighted accents, and gifts.

Need some decorating ideas? Our Christmas shop has many themed trees for every style ranging from traditional to elegant to playful. Click here to see some examples of our themed trees. Get inspiration for decorating chandeliers, mantles, and railings. And our decorators are always available to help!

You'll find a greenhouse stocked with colorful poinsettias, holly, mistletoe, fresh greens, and premium grade fraser fir trees. And our designers work on custom decorated wreaths, trees, and holiday planters.

In the gift shop you'll find collectibles such as the villages of Department 56, Jim Shore, Buyer’s Choice, Woof n’Poof, Radko, and gifts for everyone on your list: jewelry, scarves, and purses, toys, gourmet foods, kitchen gadgets, table settings, and more.
